Game Rules

How to Play

  • Choose your warrior name and difficulty level
  • Answer selected-category-related questions within the time limit
  • Use power-ups strategically to maximize your score
  • Survive all questions to achieve victory!

Lives System

  • Start with 3 lives ❤️❤️❤️
  • Lose a life for wrong answers or timeouts
  • Game ends when all lives are lost
  • Skip power-up costs 1 life but saves time

Timer

  • 30 seconds per question ⏰
  • Timer turns red in last 5 seconds
  • Faster answers = time bonus points
  • Time runs out = automatic wrong answer
🏆

Scoring System

Base Points by Difficulty

Difficulty Base Points Max Time Bonus
🟢 Easy 10 points +15 points
🟡 Medium 20 points +15 points
🔴 Hard 30 points +15 points

Bonus Calculation

  • Time Bonus = Remaining Seconds ÷ 2
  • Total Score = Base Points + Time Bonus
  • Perfect game with max time = Maximum points!
